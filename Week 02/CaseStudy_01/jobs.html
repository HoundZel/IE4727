We Are Hiring!

  • Cafe Manager

    • Oversees the day-to-day operations of the coffee house, including managing staff, inventory, and financial records.
    • Ensures a high level of customer satisfaction by maintaining a clean and efficient work environment and implementing effective strategies for improving the overall customer experience.

    Salary: $55,000 / year

  • Pastry Chef

    • Creates and prepares a variety of freshly baked pastries, cakes, and other desserts to complement the coffee menu.
    • Ensures that all baked goods are made with high-quality ingredients and presented in an appealing and visually appealing manner.

    Salary: $22/ hour

  • Barista

    • Responsible for preparing and serving high-quality coffee and espresso-based beverages to customers in a cafe or coffee shop setting.
    • Must have excellent customer service skills, extensive knowledge of coffee types and brewing methods, and the ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced environment.

    Salary: $22/ hour

  • Cashier

    • Responsible for handling cash and credit card transactions, processing customer orders, and providing friendly and efficient customer service.
    • Assists in maintaining the cleanliness and organization of the front-of-house area, including stocking and replenishing supplies as needed.

    Salary: $22/ hour

  • Barback

    • Supports the baristas by ensuring that the coffee and espresso equipment are clean, well-stocked, and in good working order.
    • Assists with tasks such as cleaning, restocking, and preparing ingredients to help the baristas focus on serving customers efficiently.

    Salary: $22/ hour

  • Janitor

    • Responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and sanitation of the entire coffee house, including the front-of-house, back-of-house, and restrooms.
    • Ensures that all surfaces, floors, and equipment are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected to uphold high standards of hygiene and safety.

    Salary: $22/ hour
